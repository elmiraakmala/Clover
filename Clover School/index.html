Clover International High School

Clover International High School offers an outstanding education for students from around the world. Our complete offering, plus our passion for excellence in education and a commitment to seeing our students succeed, is how we create bright futures for each of our students.

  1. We aim to develop principled global citizens with intercultural perspectives and a passion for lifelong learning.
  2. We practise open-mindedness and show empathy across cultures.
  3. We will work with others for the common good of the community.

International students from a diverse global community

Each year, 2700 students from around the world study at Clover International High Schools.

Our students progress through our independent school, through high school with world-class curricula that will open the door to a wide range of university and career choices. At every level, they receive the care, support and internationally recognised curriculum required to help them build a successful future.


Our Mission :

Frequently Asked Questions

Clover International High School offers an outstanding education for students from around the world, ranging from Excellence High school. This is how we create bright futures for each of our students.

  • Application process

    Applications can be done in person at one of our campuses, online, or by filling out a hard copy application form that can be emailed or posted to us.

  • Services on offer

    Each student at Clover International High School is assigned a tutor to supervise their study and provide individual guidance. As part of the learning support, student and tutor meet regularly to reflect on progress, celebrate success and overcome any challenges. Students are encouraged to develop good study habits and benefit from personalised support, to ensure they reach their goals.

  • Study in New Zealand

    If you want an exceptional international education in a unique and beautiful place, New Zealand is a great choice. Our education system is one of the best in the world, and we offer a great lifestyle full of unique opportunities and experiences.
